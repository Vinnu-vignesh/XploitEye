Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and understand how to use ExploitEye effectively.

How do I start a scan?

Navigate to the "Start Scan" section from the menu, enter the website URL, and click the Scan button. The scan will begin, and a PDF report will be generated.

What types of vulnerabilities are checked?

Our scanner checks for common issues like open ports (via Nmap), SQL Injection, XSS, missing security headers, and exposed directories.

Where can I find previous scan results?

Go to the "Scan Tracker" section in the menu to view a history of all scans performed. You can also download their respective PDF reports.

What should I do if a vulnerability is found?

Each result in the report includes a mitigation tip. For serious issues, contact your web hosting provider or security team for assistance.

Is this scanner legal to use on any website?

No. Only scan websites you own or have permission to scan. Unauthorized scanning can be illegal and unethical.
